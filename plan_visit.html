Getting to Kakran, Dhamrai

From Dhaka (Bus/Car)

Kakran is approximately 45km northwest of Dhaka. Take the Dhaka-Aricha Highway toward Dhamrai. The journey usually takes **1.5 to 2 hours** depending on traffic.

Recommended: Hire a private car or use local bus services to Nayarhat Bazaar, followed by a short rickshaw ride to the village.

Accommodation & Safety

There are limited formal lodging options in Kakran village itself. We recommend staying in nearby Dhamrai town or returning to Dhaka in the evening.

Safety Note: Always be respectful of the community's culture and ask permission before taking photographs of the potters or their homes.
