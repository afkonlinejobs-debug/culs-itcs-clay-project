The Journey from Earth to Art

1

Clay Preparation

Careful selection and kneading of the high-quality local alluvial clay to ensure perfect consistency for shaping.

2

Shaping on the Wheel

The potters' manual wheel, an inheritance of skill, is used to throw the clay into traditional forms and modern designs.

3

Sun Drying & Decoration

The pots are naturally dried under the Bangladesh sun, followed by intricate decoration and painting by the women of the village.

4

The Firing Kiln

The final, crucial step: firing the pieces in traditional, manually operated kilns to achieve durability and the distinct terracotta color.
